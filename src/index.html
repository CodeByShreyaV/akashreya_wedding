Maya and Keshal did, in fact, meet in Miami over spring break. They were with separately, but met through a common friend (Hey, Swati!). Back at Barnard/Columbia, while Keshal was courting Maya, he would dutifully walk her back to Plimpton on 121st and Amsterdam, claiming that it wasn't safe for her to walk that way by herself. That abruptly stopped the moment she agreed to start dating him :/




After college, Maya moved back to India. Keshal stayed in NYC. They did looooong distance for 2 loooong years. That was hard. But they saw each other once a year on trips they took together to Spain, Croatia and Serbia.

In 2013, Maya moved to Boston for Business School. They were still in a long distance relationship, but at least they were in the same time zone! Travel


Visas
Good news! It's super easy to get a short stay visa for Sri Lanka. Simply fill out this form (link). You should receive an approval within 24-48 hours. Be sure to print that out and bring it with you!

Arriving in Sri Lanka
Fly into Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB) - Kalutara is about 1.5 hours away by road. If you plan to arrive before the 16th or leave after the 19th morning, see our FAQ section (link) for vetted transportation providers you can use to hire a car and driver. You can also hire them to travel around Sri Lanka.

Weather
Oh by the way, its pretty darn hot in December, so channel your inner Bawa by bringing airy, tropics-friendly clothes (think: swim suits, sandals, breathable materials. Hawaiin shirts need not apply).

Local SIM Cards
The resort has WiFi but if you would like to get a local SIM card, you can do so at the airport. The rates are very reasonable and they fit your phone with a sim right there so its fairly easy. We recommend this option if you plan to travel around Sri Lanka before or after the wedding.

The Anantara Kalutara is a Geoffrey Bawa property. Who is Geoffrey Bawa you ask? He was an iconic Sri Lankan architect known for this signature "Tropical Modernism" style, defined by sunlit, breezy structures that meld into their natural surroundings. At the Anantara Kalutara, that means a lagoon on side of the hotel and an ocean on the other. The resort has beach access as well as 2 pools.
